Abstract

This paper presents the control methodology and trajectory tracking of a thrust-vectoring UAV equipped with rotatable thrusters.

To achieve omnidirectional thrust vector control, a geometric controller is employed as the high-level controller, while an exact bundled redistributed (EBR) control allocation method is proposed to accurately allocate the desired wrench commands to each actuator.

Finally, trajectory tracking simulation results are presented to validate the locomotion capabilities and effectiveness of both the omnidirectional UAV platform and the proposed control framework.
